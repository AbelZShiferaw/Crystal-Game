Crystals Collector Game

Instructions

Inside the "Master Gamer" box, you will be given a random number at the start of each game.

There are four crystal buttons. Clicking on a crystal button you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal also changes.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. And you lose if your total score goes over the random number.
